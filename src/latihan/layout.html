Investigation is a systematic process of gathering, analyzing, and interpreting information to uncover the truth or solve a specific problem. It involves planning the investigation, collecting relevant data through methods like interviews or document reviews, analyzing the information to identify patterns or key findings, and drawing conclusions based on this analysis. The results are then documented in a report, often with recommendations for further action, followed by any necessary corrective measures to resolve the issue.
